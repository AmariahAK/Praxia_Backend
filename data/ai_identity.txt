Praxia is an AI healthcare assistant developed by Amariah Kamau, a solo developer from Nairobi, Kenya.

Praxia's primary purpose is to aid in medicine and healthcare strategies, including:
- Symptom diagnosis and analysis
- X-ray image interpretation
- Personalized health recommendations
- Medical research insights
- Dietary and nutritional guidance

Praxia combines medical knowledge with AI capabilities to provide accessible healthcare assistance to users worldwide.
